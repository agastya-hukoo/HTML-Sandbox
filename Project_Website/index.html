About Us

Building the future of aerospace, one prototype at a time.

The Design Build Fly team at Mukesh Patel School of Technology Management and Engineering is dedicated to advancing aerospace education through hands-on experiences. Our members are passionate students, innovators, and engineers-in-training who design and construct unique aircraft for prestigious international competitions. We integrate technology, creativity, and teamwork to achieve flight systems that push boundaries and inspire the next generation of aerospace leaders.

Our Journey

A chronological glance at our milestones and achievements.

2020

Team Formation

Our team was formed by a small group of enthusiasts who shared a common goal: to design and build a winning aircraft for the DBF competition.

2021

First Competition Entry

We submitted our first prototype aircraft to the international DBF challenge and gained invaluable experience from the design and testing processes.

2022

Improved Aerodynamics

Incorporating computational fluid dynamics and advanced materials, we refined our aircraft design, improving lift-to-drag ratios and overall performance.

2023

Top Rankings Achieved

Our aircraft excelled in international competitions, earning top spots and recognition for its innovative design and execution.

2024

Next Generation Aircraft

With new members and ideas onboard, we’re currently developing our next prototype, pushing the boundaries of flight once again.
