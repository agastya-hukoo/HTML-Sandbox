About Us

Building the future of aerospace, one prototype at a time.

The Design Build Fly team at Mukesh Patel School of Technology Management and Engineering is dedicated to advancing aerospace education through hands-on experience. Our members are passionate innovators and engineers-in-training who design and construct unique aircraft for international competitions. Our work blends technology, creativity, and collaboration, striving to achieve excellence and inspire the next generation of aerospace pioneers.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped our path in 2024.

21 July 2024

Team Formation

A group of dedicated students came together, laying the foundation for our Design Build Fly initiative. This day marked the beginning of our pursuit to create innovative aerial solutions.

29 October 2024

Proposal Submission

After weeks of ideation and refinement, we submitted our official project proposal to the international DBF competition, outlining our aircraft design concepts and strategies.

22 November 2024

Proposal Results

Our proposal results arrived, placing us 27th globally, 3rd in Asia, and 1st in India. This recognition fueled our determination to push even harder towards our goals.

14 December 2024

Airframe Construction Begins

With the groundwork set, we moved from theory to action. Our team started the manual construction of the aircraft’s airframe, turning our designs into a tangible reality.
